Right Click to select a player capsule. Green is a healer, Red is DPS, and Blue is the Tank.
Left Click to move to the target location.
Camera is panned by moving the mouse to the edge of the screen.
Mouse wheel zooms the camera in and out.
Space bar to shoot while selecting the Tank or the DPS.
K to do burst damage while selecting the DPS.
H to heal the target beneath the cursor (Not applicable to boss or terrain) while selecting the healer.
